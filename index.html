Keyboard Shortcuts for windows

How do you quickly switch between different windows on your operating system ? what is the shortcut ?

You can switch between different windows with the shrtcut alt + tab on windows.

How do you move the insertion point to the beginning of the document / to the end of the document ?

you can use the shortcut ctrl + home to move to the beginning of a document and ctrl + end to move to the bottom of a document on windows.

how do you move the insertion pint to the beginning of current line / to the end of the line ?

by pressing home on your keyboard you move to the beginning of the line and by oressing end you move to the end of the line on windows

How do you select the text between you cursor and the end of the current line ?

you can select text from the insertion point to either end or beginning by pressing shift + end or shift + home respectively
by placing your cursor at the end or beginning and using the same shortcuts respectively you can also highlight entire paragraphs on windows.

How do you open settings in any app ?

you can press the windows logo key + i to open settings on windows.

how do you refresh a page ?

to refresh a page you press the F5 key on windows.

how do you refresh a page while emptying the cache ? why would you want to do this ?

You can refresh a page and empty the cache simultaneous by pressing ctrl + F5 on windows.
